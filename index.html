Campus life

Kendriya Vidyalaya Pathankot-CAMPUS

Kendriya Vidyalaya No. 1 (AFS) Pathankot established in the year 1967 is one of the prestigious and oldest Vidyalaya of Jammu Region located at the Air Force Station Pathankot. It is governed by Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan, an autonomous body under the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Govt. of India formed in 1963 to open and manage Kendriya Vidyalayas through the length and breadth of the country and abroad to provide uninterrupted quality education to the wards of transferable Central Govt. employees including Defencepersonnels.K.V. No 1 Pathankot runs in a picturesque surroundings.

ART CLASSROOM

To kindle a conducive learning environment on par with the best institutions worldwide, Kendriya Vidyalaya Pathankot has spared no effort or investment to ensure that the best infrastructure is made available to students to enable them to pursue their education with ease and comfort.

Smart Class - Interactive Digital classroom:

Kendriya Vidyalaya Pathankot was one of the first schools in India to have smart boards in all the classrooms and also a 5.1 theatre system in all classrooms.

Sports & games

Students are segregated into four houses namely Alphard House, Canopus House, Diadem House and Plasma House. Intra-mural games and sports are conducted to promote healthy competitions among the four houses where the adrenaline is gushed up as they take part in all events energetically.
